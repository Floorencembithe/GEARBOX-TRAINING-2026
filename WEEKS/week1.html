What We Covered

This week introduced us to the world of embedded systems — what they are, how they differ from general-purpose computers, and why they matter in the real world. We looked at examples ranging from washing machines to medical devices.

We also set up our development environments and got familiar with the tools we'll be using throughout the 12 weeks.
